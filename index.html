Tips for Success

Maximize your chances of winning with these guidelines

Presentation Tips

Focus on clarity and conciseness in your presentation

Demonstrate a working prototype or proof of concept

Highlight the unique value proposition of your solution

Practice your presentation to ensure smooth delivery

Be prepared to answer questions from the judges

Team Collaboration

Clearly define roles and responsibilities for each team member

Leverage each member's strengths and expertise

Maintain effective communication throughout the process

Practice your presentation as a team to ensure cohesion

Technical Excellence

Focus on clean, well-documented code

Implement proper error handling and edge cases

Use version control effectively (Git)

Test your solution thoroughly before presentation
